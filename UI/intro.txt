# Marine Creatures Home
This project demonstrates the [Marine Creature Recognition in Aerial Video Project](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1BzOS1zjk1zuBGZ80LHrMJxwOzhnLLsTUtDQF0O05W_4/edit#heading=h.vzciyciifgh3) into an interactive [Dash](https://plotly.com/dash/) app. 
Using PyTorch and Labelbox

Sponsored by Center for Coastal Marine Sciences and Cal Poly San Luis Obispo
In collaboration with the Shark Lab @ CSULB
